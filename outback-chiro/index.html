How We Can Help

Conditions We Treat

Our team is experienced in diagnosing and treating a wide range of musculoskeletal conditions. If you're in pain or discomfort, we can help.

💪

Back Pain

Lower back pain, upper back pain, disc issues — we'll identify the cause and get you on the road to recovery.

🧠

Neck Pain

Stiffness, restricted movement, or acute pain in the neck from poor posture, injury, or stress.

😬

Headaches & Migraines

Tension headaches and migraines often have a musculoskeletal origin that responds well to chiropractic care.

⚡

Sciatica

Shooting pain from the lower back down the leg? We treat the underlying cause, not just the symptoms.

🏃

Sports Injuries

From weekend warriors to elite athletes — we help you recover faster and perform better.

🚗

Motor Accident Injuries

Whiplash and other injuries from motor vehicle accidents treated with comprehensive care.

🤰

Pregnancy Related Pain

Gentle, safe treatment for the unique musculoskeletal challenges that pregnancy brings.

💼

Workplace Injuries

Repetitive strain, postural problems, and work-related injuries — we accept workers compensation claims.

What We Offer

Our Services

Chiropractic Adjustments

We employ a wide range of established techniques including Gonstead, Diversified, Thompson drop, SOT, and Activator — tailored to your specific needs.

Sports Chiropractic

Dr Haslett holds a postgraduate International Chiropractic Sports Science Diploma, bringing elite-level sports injury knowledge to your care.

Rehabilitation

Both passive and active rehabilitation programs tailored to facilitate healing and ensure you're less likely to suffer re-injuries or relapses.

Posture Analysis & Correction

A postural screening can reveal the underlying health and shape of your spine, helping prevent issues before they start.

Massage Therapy

Licensed massage therapists using kneading, compression, and controlled pressure to relieve chronic stress and muscle tension.

Lifestyle & Ergonomic Advice

Exercises, posture correction, ergonomic guidance, dietary advice, and best practices to keep you well between visits.
